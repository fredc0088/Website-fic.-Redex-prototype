Redex is music school based in South East London, specialised in short instrument courses.

This the right structure for anyone looking for an artistic journey that meets your needs:

  • Music's full spectrum with targets ranging from amateur to expert.
  • Stimulating the passion and the desire to express yourself in a conscious way.

Redex is based on three major objectives:

  • Convey the pleasure of making music;
  • Enhance the art as a factor in individual and social growth, stimulating the intelligence, creativity and responsibility;
